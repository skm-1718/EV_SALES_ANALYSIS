# EV_SALES_ANALYSIS

The data visualization analysis indicates that India's Electric Vehicle market is experiencing steady growth, with 2023 recording the highest sales and Uttar Pradesh emerging as the leading state in EV sales. Sales were highest during November, followed by December and January, suggesting strong seasonal demand

Goal

Analyze EV sales trends to identify growth opportunities and support business decisions.

Objectives

• Analyze year-wise sales

• Compare state performance

• Evaluate vehicle categories

• Study monthly trends

• Discover market insights


Key Insights

• Strong growth after 2021

• Uttar Pradesh leads EV sales

• 2-Wheelers dominate the market

• Seasonal monthly demand exists


Business Recommendations

• Expand charging infrastructure

• Focus on top-selling states

• Strengthen 2W and 3W segments

• Apply predictive analytics


Conclusion

India's EV market is expanding rapidly, offering significant opportunities for manufacturers,
policymakers, and investors through data-driven planning.
